Credit Horizon combines the cutting edge technology of artificial intelligence and security of blockchain to create a unique algorithm designed to provide the most accurate credit score possible to our users. Unlike traditional credit scores, which are both unclear and fixed, putting a large population at a major disadvantage, our company uses a revolutionary new method that not only looks at a users past but takes into account the context and potential of the user. Included in this is a modern approach to starting credit scores which gives new users a six-month trial period from which the initial credit score is determined as well as a formula that calculates the credit score by assigning weights to different categories specific to each person. By including such features, we hope to pave the way for a secure, technological-centered, and inclusive future.

Blockstack is a full-stack decentralized computing network that enables a new generation of applications where developers and users can interact fairly securely. Blockstack achieves the purpose of suppressing fraud and authenticating the user. Blockstack uses a dedicated Blockchain, designed for decentralized apps, to provide Blockstack IDs and execute smart-contracts

To make sure that the user data is stored in a secure location, we implemented Gaia through Blockstack. Gaia is a decentralized high-performance storage system. It works by hosting data in one or more existing storage systems of the users choice. Access control in a Gaia storage hub is performed on a per-address basis.
